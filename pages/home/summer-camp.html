8 Courses — Pick Your Passion
Each session runs 3 hours — mornings (9:30–12:30) or afternoons (1:00–4:00).
Sessions are self-contained; register for one week or mix across the whole summer.
Beginner · Level 2 🎮
Game Design with Scratch
A STEM introduction using Scratch software. Students design and build their own games every day, developing logic, creativity, and computational thinking from the ground up.
Ages 12–16 Weeks 2, 4, 6 9:30am–12:30pm
Intermediate · Level 3 🖨
3D Print & Design
Hands-on tutorials covering basic 3D modeling and product design concepts. Students design real objects and bring them to life using Hacker Dojo's 3D printers.
Ages 12–17 Weeks 1, 4, 7, 9, 11 AM or PM
Beginner–Intermediate 🪡
Fashion & Textile Design
Machine sewing fundamentals leading to a final runway showcase. Students learn to design, cut, sew, and present original garments — from sketch to catwalk.
Ages 12–17 Weeks 1, 7, 9 AM or PM
Intermediate · Level 2 🤖
LEGO Robotics Space Camp
Solar system–themed projects that combine LEGO building with programming concepts, emphasizing teamwork and 21st-century skills through space exploration challenges.
Ages 12–16 Weeks 2, 6 AM or PM
Intermediate–Advanced ⚙
VEX IQ Engineering & Game Challenge
Students build and program VEX IQ robots, applying sensor logic and engineering design to compete in structured game challenges — a serious intro to competitive robotics.
Ages 12–17 Weeks 3, 11 AM or PM
Intermediate · Level 3 🕹
Game Design with Godot
Video game creation using the Godot 4 engine — the open-source professional tool used by indie studios worldwide. Students design levels, code mechanics, and ship playable games.
Ages 14–17 Weeks 3, 8, 10, 12 AM or PM
Intermediate · Level 2 🥊
Robotics SumoBot Engineering
Engineering design for competitive robot challenges. Students design, build, and program SumoBots to compete in arena battles — applying physics, electronics, and coding strategy.
Ages 12–16 Week 8 1:00–4:00pm
Intermediate–Advanced 🎭
Prop Making for Cosplay & Movies
Multi-material prop creation for cosplay and film. Students combine foam, thermoplastics, paint, and electronics to build professional-quality props from concept to finished piece.
Ages 12–17 Weeks 10, 12 AM or PM
