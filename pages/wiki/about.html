About Hacker Dojo
A 501(c)(3) nonprofit built by the Silicon Valley tech community, for the tech community.
Mission
"Provide the tech community and other communities in Silicon Valley an affordable place to learn, collaborate and innovate so that anyone from any circumstance and background with an idea can build their dream."

Hacker Dojo is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit established in 2009, headquartered in Mountain View, California. We bring together entrepreneurs, programmers, creators, and other hackers — using the positive interpretation of "hacker" as someone fascinated by how things work and driven to build new things.

Mission Components
  • Providing affordable space for learning, collaboration, and innovation
  • Advancing entrepreneurial vision
  • Encouraging disruption and innovation
  • Functioning as a hub for technology leaders and contributors
  • Enabling communities through educational, collaborative, and social initiatives
History

Hacker Dojo emerged in 2009 from several founders with aligned visions for a community programming center in the heart of Silicon Valley:

  • David Weekly conceived a community programming center
  • Jeff Lindsay drew inspiration from SuperHappyDevHouse
  • Brian Klug had previously pursued a space on Castro Street
  • Early organizers included Dean Mao, Nathan Schmidt, and Zonker

The founding group — fluctuating between 10–30 members — held regular meetings at PBworks HQ in San Mateo, using mailing lists to coordinate. Early discussions covered space acquisition, corporate structure (LLC vs. Corporation), and tax designation (501(c)(3) vs. 501(c)(6)). The 501(c)(3) path was chosen to best serve the community's educational and charitable mission.

Community Guidelines
  • Practice flexibility and openness
  • Treat others with excellence
  • Maintain cleanliness — leave spaces better than you found them
  • Offer assistance proactively
  • New members should introduce themselves to at least three people
  • Get your photo taken by the Mural
  • Explore events beyond your usual interests
  • Promote Hacker Dojo on social channels
