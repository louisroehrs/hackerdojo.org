Visitors
How to sign in, connect, and make the most of your visit.
Open Monday–Friday, 10am–9pm. A $10 donation is suggested. Members receive 24/7 access after signing up and receiving a key.
Step 1 — Sign In

Find the sign-in laptop near the front desk and register your email address. This helps us track visitor numbers without generating excessive email. It only takes a moment.

Step 2 — Grab a Seat

Multiple spaces offer open seating: the main area, the event space, and the classroom. The environment is friendly — feel free to introduce yourself and ask about what others are working on, or just settle in quietly and get to work. The kitchen has coffee and tea if you need fuel.

Step 3 — Connect to WiFi

The HD-Guest network is available to all visitors with no password. You'll need to agree to the Hacker Dojo Policies through the splash screen. Guest WiFi provides one day of internet access per seven-day period.

Need faster internet? A $20 day pass gives you access to the 1-gigabit member network. See WiFi Help if you have connection issues.

Step 4 — Explore & Connect

Regular classes and events serve as great social icebreakers. Friday Happy Hour Lightning Talks are a particularly good time to meet people and hear what others are building.

When to Visit
Peak Hours
Around 7pm on weekdays. Most people are here, energy is high, conversations flow naturally.
Daytime
Quieter and better for focused work. Fewer people but still active with members working on projects.
Weekends
Highly variable — could be empty or packed with 150+ people depending on weather and scheduled events. Check the calendar first.
