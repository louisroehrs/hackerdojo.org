Running Events
Everything event organizers need to know — from submission to teardown.
Before organizing an event, review the full Hacker Dojo Event Policies. Organizers are responsible for everything from setup to cleanup to attendee sign-in.
Before the Event
Communication to Attendees

Make sure your attendees know:

  • Directions to 855 Maude Ave, Mountain View, CA 94043
  • Entry procedures — where to sign in and how to get in after hours
  • Parking locations (green zones only)
  • Restroom locations
  • WiFi networks: HD-Events or HD-Guest
Social Promotion

When promoting your event, reference Hacker Dojo on social platforms:

  • Twitter/X: @HackerDojo
  • Facebook: @hackerdojo
  • Instagram: @hackerdojo

Do not advertise externally until your event has been officially approved.

Setup
  • Maximum 1 hour setup time — overlaps with the previous event's teardown
  • Organizer locates and arranges all projectors, tables, chairs, and supplies themselves
  • Room arrangement is the organizer's responsibility
During the Event
  • Organizer must remain present for the full duration
  • Attendees park only in designated green zones; large events may require a parking coordinator
  • All attendees must sign in at the front desk or sign-in station
  • Encourage attendees to donate or consider membership
After the Event
Teardown & Cleanup
  • Maximum 1 hour teardown (shared with next event's setup)
  • Return room to the posted standard configuration (photo posted in each room)
  • Organizer is responsible for removing all excess trash
  • Large events must refill communal kitchen items (cups, bowls, glasses)
  • Power down all projectors and electrical equipment before leaving
Damage Responsibility
Organizers are liable for any facility damage that occurs during their event. Treat the space with care and report any issues immediately.
Cancellations & Changes
  • Room changes require 2-day notice; staff will prioritize attendee accommodation
  • If cancelling, update both the Hacker Dojo system and any external platforms (Meetup, Eventbrite, etc.)
