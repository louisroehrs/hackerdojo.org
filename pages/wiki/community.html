Community
Initiatives, events, and programs created by Hacker Dojo members and volunteers.
These pages are created by the community, volunteers, and event organizers. Want to add your initiative? Reach out — we're actively growing this section.
Community Initiatives
HAM Radio
Amateur radio in and around Mountain View, along with related electronics and RF topics. A long-running community within the Dojo.
Music Corner
Instruments and controllers ready to rock out. We have a collection of gear available for members to jam on and explore.
Meditation Sessions
Quick breaks in the day to help you focus and destress. Short guided sessions open to all members and visitors.
Friday Night Socials

Probably the most popular recurring event since rebooting out of the pandemic. Friday Night Socials are a relaxed gathering where members meet, share what they're working on, and connect with newcomers.

Interested in helping host? Connect with one of our organizers to get involved and get the inside scoop on how it runs.

Discord Community

The Hacker Dojo Discord is where members coordinate projects, ask questions, share events, and stay connected throughout the week.
