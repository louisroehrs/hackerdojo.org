Network Policies
Rules and technical specifications for access points and WiFi use at Hacker Dojo.
ACCESS POINT FOR TESTING PURPOSES ONLY. Internet access for your devices should use Hacker Dojo WiFi, not your own access point.
Access Point Rules
Guests

Guests cannot establish their own access points at the facility. Use the HD-Guest network.

Members

Members may set up access points exclusively for testing purposes related to their work. Your devices' internet access should still go through Hacker Dojo WiFi — not through your AP.

Technical Requirements

If you're setting up a member testing AP, you must follow these specifications to avoid interfering with the facility network:

Channel Width
Use only 20 MHz channels. 40 MHz and 80 MHz channels create interference with the facility WiFi.
Transmit Power
Limit to 3 dB maximum. Higher power output would exceed the strength of our access points and degrade the network for everyone.
2.4 GHz Band
Use Channel 11 only.
Ch 11
5 GHz Band
Allowed channels:
52 54 60 64 100 104 108 112 136 140 144
Enforcement
Non-compliance may result in loss of personal access point privileges and potential membership termination.
