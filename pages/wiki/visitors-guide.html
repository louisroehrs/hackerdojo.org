Visitors Guide
Everything you need to know for your first visit to Hacker Dojo.
We welcome visitors every day! We're open 10am–9pm daily. No membership required to visit — just show up.
Welcome

Hacker Dojo is a collaborative space where tech enthusiasts, entrepreneurs, and builders work side by side. Visitors are welcome every day, typically 10am to 9pm. A $10 donation is appreciated for drop-in visits.

Day passes ($20) give you access to the faster 1-gigabit member internet, though they don't include event or lab access. Membership provides 24/7 keycard access and full use of all facilities.

Facilities Overview

The Dojo is roughly split between workspace and social areas:

  • Desks and workbenches for focused work
  • Couches and social areas for collaboration
  • Break room with coffee enthusiasts and kitchen access
  • Electronics lab with oscilloscopes, power supplies, and a 100-watt CO2 laser cutter
Virtual Tours

Can't visit in person? Three telepresence robots let you explore remotely:

  • Mo Bot
  • Larry Bot
  • Curly Bot

Call into any available charged unit to navigate the space and interact with members in real time.

Visiting Tips
Best Times
  • Peak activity: Around 3pm
  • Quieter mornings: Preferred if you want fewer distractions
  • Weekends: Variable — check the event calendar
  • Friday evenings: Socializing and drink-mixing events
Meeting People

Many visitors are working on laptops and are open to brief conversation. The easiest icebreaker: introduce yourself and ask "What are you working on?" Classes and events are also great entry points into the community.

Community Motto
"Be Excellent to each other!"
