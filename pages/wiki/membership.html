What's Included

Hacker Dojo is a place where you can work and sometimes play, away from the distractions of home or the office. Members are encouraged to interact with each other and make the most of the community.

Space & Access
  • 24/7 access via digital keycard — no front desk required
  • Public desks for members and guests
  • Private meeting room reservations
Equipment & Labs
  • Electronics lab — well-equipped maker space
  • Makerspace tools and machines (after completing required training)
  • Monitors and keyboards available for use
Connectivity & Amenities
  • High-speed fiber internet and power outlets
  • Complimentary coffee, tea, water, and occasional beverages
Events & Community
  • Free attendance at all member-run events
  • Ability to create and host your own member events
  • Free participation in offered classes and workshops
  • Technical networking and social activities
  • Social events including tournaments and Friday Night Socials
  • Access to the job board with startup listings
Cancellation & Holds
No refunds. Hacker Dojo does not refund membership dues. With monthly billing, you can cancel before the next charge date to avoid renewal.

Members can suspend or terminate membership through the billing tab at dashboard.hackerdojo.com. Make sure to cancel before your billing cycle renews.
