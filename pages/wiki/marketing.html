Marketing & Branding
Official logo, colors, naming standards, and guidelines for using the Hacker Dojo brand.
Official Name
Full Name
Hacker Dojo — two words, capital H and capital D. Always.
Shortened Form
the Dojo — Dojo is always capitalized when used as a shortened name.
Official Colors
Red
PANTONE 1945 C
Primary brand color
Black
Primary text and logo on light backgrounds
White
Logo and text on dark backgrounds

Note: The website uses a slightly different red (#e13838 / #df3f33) for digital applications. The PANTONE 1945 C specification is for print and merchandise.

Logo
Main Logo
Standard logo for most applications. Torii icon + "Hacker Dojo" wordmark. Available as Adobe Illustrator and 1200×2400 Black Text PNG.
Dark on Light
White text version for use on dark backgrounds. Available as Adobe Illustrator and 1732×4905 White Text PNG.
Icon Only
Torii symbol without wordmark. For small sizes, favicons, and avatars. Available as Adobe Illustrator and PNG.
Logo font: Whitney Medium — for reconstruction and design use only. Do not substitute other fonts.
Logo Usage Rules
  • Keep the logo intact — do not modify, skew, stretch, or alter colors
  • Do not add adjacent text, change orientation, or alter the design in any way
  • Maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the icon on all sides
  • Logo placement alongside related partner logos is generally acceptable
  • Merchandise for profit requires approval via the Sponsorships contact
Event & Activity Naming
Correct Usage
The public may use the name and logo when the relationship between parties is clear.
✓ "Bunny Fair at Hacker Dojo" — attendees understand this is an event hosted at the space.
Incorrect Usage
✗ "Hacker Dojo Bunny Fair" — this implies Hacker Dojo is organizing or endorsing the event.
Event Title Rule
Event titles cannot include "Hacker Dojo" without Director approval. Attendees must understand that members host events — not the organization itself.
